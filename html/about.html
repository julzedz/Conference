"Hello, Sharing World!" AUGMENTED REALITY ART SUMMIT 2022

As a year of technological shifts, 2022 changed the art and metaverse industry with new trends, synergies, and key players. Creatives increasingly understood the importance of collaboration, going beyond the obvious and using new mediums in their work, such as AR, VR, NFTs, etc. The AR Art Summit was born as a reflection of the current shifts happening in the art world.
