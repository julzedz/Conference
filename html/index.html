"Hello, Sharing World!" AUGMENTED REALITY ART SUMMIT 2022

Join us for a wonderful panel discussion that will focus on the usage of augmented reality in different industries and projects - from branding and packaging to raising cultural awareness and business opportunities.

2022.09.15 (WED) ~ 17 (SAT)

@ National Museum of Korea, Art Center Nabi, and more.
